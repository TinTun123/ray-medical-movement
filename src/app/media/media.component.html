First-aid Kits & Medic backpack preparation for on ground trainings

"When someone get injured, the closest person become a doctor."

The video describes the memory of on-ground trainings and first aid kits preparations in Yangon, Mon, Kayin and Kayah (Karenni). These are the sample of First aid kits & Medic backpacks that we supply after our training. They were designed to become more compact & systematic in order that everyone could help and save traumatized people near them.
